Anesthesia Fee per case $110 National Average Medicare Reimbursement for anesthesia during endoscopy is based on CPT 00801 (anesthesia code): 5 Base Units and CPT 00810 (anesthesia for lower intestinal endoscopic procedures, endoscope introduced distal to duodenum): 5 Base Units and $21.92 2013 National anesthesia conversion factor = $110 payment. Only applicable if a secondary provider delivers anesthesia during EUS. Source: 2013 Medicare Physician Final Rule.
Pathology Reimbursement $99 National Average Medicare Reimbursement for Pathology is estimated based on the 2013 Medicare HOPPS Final Rule payment w/o geographic adjustment for APC343 (CPT 88305-26 Level IV surgical pathology): $25. Assuming that the pathologist is a hospital employee, the most recent Medicare physician fee schedule for CPT 88305-26 (pathologist's interpretation): $24. Exclude this amount if the pathologist is not a hospital employee. It is assumed one case will result in two biopsies: ($25 + $24) x 2 = $99. Source: 2013 Medicare Physician and Hospital Outpatient Final Rules.
Surgery Reimbursement $8,322 Average Medicare Reimbursement for Surgery is based on the 2013 Medicare MS-DRG 328 (Stomach, Esophageal and Diodenal Proc w/o CC/MCC); Weight 1.4413, multiplied by 2013 National Inpatient Prospective Payment System Average Base rate excluding capital add-on ($5,774) = Payment $8,322. Source: 2013 Medicare Acute Inpatient PPS Final Rule.
Admissions Reimbursement $5,774 National average (wage index greater than one) of the most common GI DRG rates is calculated by using the national adjusted full update standardized labor, non-labor and capital amounts. Source: MM8041 Revised January 4, 2013. Customers are encouraged to enter site-specific estimates based on claims data.
Oncology Reimbursement $4,549 Average Medicare Inpatient Reimbursement for Oncology is estimated based on the 2013 Medicare MS-DRG 848 (Chemotherapy w/o acute leukemia as secondary diagnosis w/0 CC/MCC): Weight (0.7878) multiplied by 2013 National Inpatient Prospective Payment System Average Base rate excluding capital add on ($5,774) = Payment $10,388. Source 2013 Medicare Acute Inpatient PPS Final Rule.
Radiology Reimbursment $81 National Average Medicare Reimbursement for Radiology is estimated based on the average APC 276 (Level I Digestive Radiology) payment - $81. Source: 2013 Medicare Hospital Outpatient Final Rule.
Anesthesia - Non Endoscopy Reimbursement $154 National Average Medicare Reimbursement for Anesthesia is estimated based on the 2013 Medicare Physician fee schedule for CPT 00790 (anesthesia for intraperitoneal procedures in upper abdomen incl. laparoscopy). 7 base units multiplied by 2013 National anesthesia conversion factor of $21.52 = Payment of $154. Source: 2013 Medicare Physician Final Rule.
